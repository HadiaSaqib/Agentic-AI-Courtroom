Construction and maintenance:
  - Vehicles must conform to construction and maintenance rules under Chapter VI; selling or altering vehicles to render them unlawful is penalized.
Tyres:
  - Vehicles must be fitted with pneumatic tyres unless specifically prescribed otherwise.
Defective vehicles:
  - Driving a vehicle with defects known or discoverable by ordinary care is an offence; penalties escalate if accidents occur.
Permits & special registrations:
  - Heavy transport vehicles and special classes may require permits and certificates of fitness; conditions may restrict routes/uses.
Weights:
  - Adhere to unladen/laden and axle weight limits on registration certificate.
Equipment:
  - Prescribed mechanical/electrical signalling devices must be fitted and operational for certain vehicle types (e.g., left-hand control).
