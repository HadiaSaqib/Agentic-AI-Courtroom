- Motor vehicle: vehicle as defined in the Ordinance.
- Transport vehicle: any motor vehicle used for carriage of passengers or goods for hire/reward requiring special permits/fitness.
- Licence: driving licence issued by licensing authority; may include authorisations for public service vehicles.
- Fitness certificate: certificate required for transport vehicles confirming mechanical fitness.
- Permit: authorisation for certain vehicles/uses/routes, issued by Regional/Provincial Transport Authorities.
- Mandatory traffic sign: sign in Part-III of Ninth Schedule with red border/ground for regulatory purposes.
- Crash helmet: helmet meeting prescribed material and safety requirements; obligatory for two-wheeler riders and pillion.
- Weighment: process of measuring laden/unladen/axle weights to ensure compliance with registration specs.
