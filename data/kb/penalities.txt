Driving without licence:
  - Imprisonment up to 6 months OR fine up to Rs. 500 OR both.
  - If licence is for transport vehicle: imprisonment up to 2 years AND fine up to Rs. 1000.

Driving at excessive speed (section 98):
  - Fine up to Rs. 100.
  - If vehicle is a transport vehicle: fine not less than Rs. 100 and up to Rs. 500.

Employer causing employee to speed:
  - Fine up to Rs. 200 (up to Rs. 500 for transport vehicles).

Dangerous driving (section 99):
  - Imprisonment up to 6 months OR fine up to Rs. 500 OR both.
  - If transport vehicle: imprisonment up to 1 year AND fine up to Rs. 1000.
  - Repeat offence within 3 years: imprisonment up to 2 years OR fine up to Rs. 1000; transport vehicle repeat up to 4 years and fine up to Rs. 1000.

Driving under influence (section 100):
  - Imprisonment up to 6 months OR fine up to Rs. 1000 OR both.
  - Repeat: imprisonment up to 2 years OR fine up to Rs. 1000 OR both.

Driving when unfit due to disease/disability (section 101):
  - Fine up to Rs. 200. Repeat: fine up to Rs. 500.

Abetment of offences (section 102):
  - Punishable with same punishment as the principal offence.

Racing and trials (section 103):
  - Imprisonment up to 6 months OR fine up to Rs. 1000 OR both.

Using unsafe vehicle (section 104):
  - Imprisonment up to 1 month OR fine up to Rs. 500 OR both.
  - If accident with injury/damage: imprisonment up to 6 months OR fine up to Rs. 1000 OR both.

Selling/altering vehicle unlawfully (section 105):
  - Fine up to Rs. 200.

Using vehicle without permit (section 106):
  - Imprisonment up to 6 months OR fine up to Rs. 500 OR both.
  - Repeat: imprisonment up to 2 years OR fine up to Rs. 1000 OR both.

Contravention of weight/permit/section 76/78 (section 107):
  - Fine up to Rs. 100; repeat: fine up to Rs. 500.

Contravening duties in accident/false info (section 108):
  - Imprisonment up to 6 months OR fine up to Rs. 1000 OR both.

Taking vehicle without authority (section 109):
  - Imprisonment up to 3 months OR fine up to Rs. 500 OR both.

Unauthorized interference with vehicle (section 110):
  - Imprisonment up to 1 month OR fine up to Rs. 200 OR both.

Disobedience/obstruction/false info (section 111):
  - Fine up to Rs. 200.

Contravention of appliance rules (section 111-A):
  - Fine up to Rs. 500 and forfeiture of appliance.

General provision (section 112):
  - Fine up to Rs. 100; repeat: fine up to Rs. 500.

Power of arrest (section 113):
  - Police may arrest without warrant for offences under sections 99, 100, 110.

Summary disposal (section 116, 116-A):
  - On-the-spot charges and fines for schedule offences (varies: Rs. 10/20; summary payment removes further proceedings where applicable).

Scale of compensation (13th schedule):
  - Death of passenger in stage/contract carriage: Rs. 16,000.
  - Various injury compensations listed (e.g., loss of arm Rs. 5,000, loss of eye Rs. 6,000, etc.).
