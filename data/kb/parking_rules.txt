Designated parking:
  - Government/local authority can determine where vehicles may stand indefinitely or for specified times.
Public service halting:
  - Public service vehicles may be allowed longer halting times at designated stands.
Removal of signs/obstructions:
  - Government may remove signs/ads obscuring traffic signs or deceptive signs that resemble traffic signs.
Parking offences:
  - Leaving vehicle in dangerous/obstructive position subject to penalties (see offences/penalties).
